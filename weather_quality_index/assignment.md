# Water Quality Assessment Assignment

## Overview

Access to clean water is a fundamental necessity, yet water quality can vary significantly based on environmental, geographical, and human factors. The dataset includes water quality monitoring data from across India, with chemical and physical parameters measured at various locations over different years (2019, 2020, 2021, 2022).

## Dataset Description

The dataset contains the following columns:

**Geographical Identifiers:**
- Well_ID, State, District, Block, Village: Geographical identifiers of water sampling locations.

**Spatial Coordinates:**
- Latitude, Longitude: Spatial coordinates for precise mapping.

**Temporal Information:**
- Year: The year in which the sample was recorded.

**Water Quality Indicators:**
- pH, Electrical Conductivity (EC), Carbonates (CO3), Bicarbonates (HCO3), Chlorides (Cl), Sulfates (SO4), Nitrates (NO3), Total Hardness (TH), Calcium (Ca), Magnesium (Mg), Sodium (Na), Potassium (K), Fluoride (F), Total Dissolved Solids (TDS)

## Target Variables

**Water Quality Index (WQI):** A numerical representation of overall water quality derived from multiple parameters.

## Objectives

1. Perform exploratory data analysis to understand water quality patterns across regions and years
2. Identify key factors influencing water quality
3. Build predictive models to estimate Water Quality Index
4. Generate insights for water quality management and policy recommendations
